General Instructions :


1. Total duration of examination is 180 minutes, The 1st section will be of Physics & Chemistry and 2nd will be of Mathematics.

2. Marks per question will be as follow :

Physics : 1Q = 1 Marks || Chemistry : 1Q = 1 Marks || Mathematics : 1Q = 2 Marks

3. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You will not be required to end or submit your examination.

4. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols :

You have not visited the question yet.

You have not answered the question.

You have answered the question.

5. Note that option will be saved only after clicking either "Save & Next" or "Save & Previous" button.

6. Submit button will appear on the last question.

7. Exam will start immediately after clicking the button "Start Test".
